# 🌸 ReBloom

ReBloom is a postpartum recovery app designed to support mothers emotionally and physically after childbirth, while creating a strong, connected healing journey between the mother and her partner.

---

## 💡 Inspiration

Postpartum recovery is one of the most overlooked yet emotionally and physically intense phases in a woman’s life.

ReBloom was created to:
- Support mothers through healing
- Strengthen emotional connection between partners
- Encourage active involvement during recovery

---

## ✨ Features

### 👩 Mother Interface

The mother interface is divided into three core sections:

#### 💞 Us (Connection Space)
- Share mood and emotional state
- Send and receive **love notes & heart notes**
- Track shared **missions**
- Monitor the **bond meter**
- Stay emotionally connected with partner

#### 🫙 Memory Jar
- Capture and store special moments with the baby
- Save memories through notes and voice
- Build a meaningful timeline of early motherhood

#### 🌿 Heal
- Access guided recovery exercises
- Includes breathing, relaxation, and gentle physical healing
- Supports postpartum body recovery

---

### 🤝 Partner Interface

The partner interface mirrors support, awareness, and learning:

#### 💞 Us (Connection Space)
- View mother’s mood and emotional updates
- Understand physical and emotional changes
- Track sent **missions, love notes, and heart notes**
- Stay actively involved in recovery

#### 🫙 Memory Jar
- View memories shared by the mother
- Share moments and participate in memory building

#### 📚 Learn
- Learn about postpartum recovery
- Understand emotional and physical challenges
- Become a more supportive and informed partner

---

## 🧠 What Makes ReBloom Unique

- Focuses on **postpartum recovery**, not just baby care
- Builds a **shared emotional space** between mother and partner
- Encourages **active participation**, not passive support
- Combines:
  - Emotional bonding ❤️
  - Physical healing 🌿
  - Memory building 🫙

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- SwiftUI
- Swift Playgrounds (.swiftpm)
- Structured data models
- Voice recording & playback

---

## 🎯 Goal

To ensure that no mother feels alone during recovery, and every partner knows how to support, understand, and grow together in this journey.
